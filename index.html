About

Meet Bunya

Bunya is a pixel artist with a decade of graphic design experience. His perseverance and stamina to learn new things has no limits.

Creative Path

Starting with Photoshop, then trying out illustrations and video editing, Bunya found joy in creating movement. He sharpened these skills at local design studios before discovering his true love for pixel art.

NFT Journey

In 2021, Bunya stepped into the exciting world of NFTs. This opened new doors, helping him connect with other artists and grow his network.

Looking Ahead

Now, Bunya is exploring programming to add a new layer to his pixel art. It's a big change, but he's excited about the possibilities it brings to his work.
